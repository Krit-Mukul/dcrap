Our Story

Founded in 2023, DCrap started with a simple observation: people wanted to recycle but found the process inconvenient and unrewarding. Traditional scrap dealers were difficult to reach, offered unclear pricing, and the whole experience was often frustrating.

We decided to change that. By leveraging technology, we created a platform that makes selling scrap as easy as ordering food online. Today, we're proud to serve thousands of customers across Punjab and beyond, helping them earn money while making a positive environmental impact.
