Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started?

Simply download the app, register with your phone number, and book your first pickup. It takes less than 3 minutes!

What types of scrap do you accept?

We accept newspapers, cardboard, plastic, metal, e-waste, glass, books, and batteries. Check our rates page for full details.

Is there a minimum quantity required?

Yes, minimum quantities vary by material type. Typically 5-10 kg for most items. Check individual item details in the app.

How do I receive payment?

Payment is credited instantly to your in-app wallet after pickup. You can withdraw to your bank account anytime.
